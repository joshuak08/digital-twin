Water Treatment Digital Twin

By: University of Bristol Students

Github Repository

Description:

Water treatment plants need a tool which can help improve the planning, design, construction and operation of their plants. A digital twin of a water system will provide accurate and reliable data of that can used to make informed decisions throughout the lifecycle of that water system.
